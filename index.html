Introduction

Shahmeer Construction is owned and managed by a team of well experienced professional engineers dealing with all kinds of contracts for construction.

Shahmeer Construction was established in May 1998. "Mian Shahid Iqbal" is the Chief Executive of the organization. He served as Site Engineer/Project Manager in Pakistan. As an Engineer he has completed many civil & mechanical engineering projects, Housing colonies, Industrial buildings and Power Plants.

Shahmeer Construction has completed many projects such as housing colonies, A-class residences, Plazas buildings, Infrastructure works, Industrial buildings, and also renovation works. Since the inception of the company it has been attained and maintained ever rising standards of performance.

Shahmeer Construction engaged in construction, contracting & property development.
